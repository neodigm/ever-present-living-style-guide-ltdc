Pattern: Primary Banner Component

Problem

The banners are presented inconsistently across the site. Individual pages embed the imagery differently and the content itself is inconsistent. Today some pages have no vertical white-space between the header and the banner and others have inconsistent horizontal margins. Maintaining the banners individually has been tedious and error prone. Banners are not distinguishable to assistive technologies.

Solution

Establish a standard, reusable and consistent component for rendering the primary banner. Standardize the content within the targeted content area.

Reference

A11y BEM Place Holder

    <aside   class="prime-banner--aside columns small-10"> </aside>
    <figure  class="prime-banner--figure columns small-10"
    aria-label="Primary Banner">
         <!-- Content Begin -->
         <img class="prime-banner--img" src="" alt="" title="">

    </figure>
</section>
